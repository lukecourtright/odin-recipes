Lasagna

A healthy slice of lasagna.

This receipe is designed to yield a Garfield-approved quality of lasagna. Is Garfield even Italian?

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Pasta sauce
  • Ground meat of choice
  • Ri-goat

Steps

  1. Cook dat meat.
  2. Mix the meat and the sauce.
  3. Lay a sheet down.
  4. Lay down some of the meat sauce
  5. Layer some ri-goat over the meat sauce
  6. Layer with a sheet on top
  7. Keep repeating until you've used everything up.
  8. Bake at four hundo for 50 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
