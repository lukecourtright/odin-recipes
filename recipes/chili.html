Chili

Dat bowl doe.

Not Left-Eye. Not T-Boz. This is the easy chili recipe for the people.

Ingredients

  • Can of black beans
  • Can of red beans
  • Ground beef
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Yellow onion
  • Chili seasoning
  • Bell pepper of choice
  • Cheese to top with

Steps

  1. Cook dat meat (use some seasoning here). Drain and throw it in the slow cooker.
  2. Cut up the pepper and throw it in the slow cooker.
  3. Cut up the onion and throw it in the slow cooker.
  4. Throw the tomatoes in.
  5. Throw the beans in.
  6. Low and slow for 8 hours, baby.
  7. Bone apple tea!
