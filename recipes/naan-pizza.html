Naan Pizza

Melding Italian with South Asian cuisine.

We had some naan and some sauce. Here we are.

Ingredients

  • Piece of naan (hopefully big)
  • Pasta sauce
  • Italian cheese blend, depending on how crazy you're feeling
  • Sausage? You can do toppings. Dealer's choice.

Steps

  1. Lay the naan down.
  2. Lay the sauce down.
  3. Lay the cheese down.
  4. Top if you want.
  5. Bake at four hundo for 30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
